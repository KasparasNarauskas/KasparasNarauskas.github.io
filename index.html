Second-year animation student at WDKA Rotterdam, working toward a career as a story artist. My focus is storyboards, animatics, and concept art — building narrative through camera, staging, and light.

Alongside story work I develop FX animation as a secondary discipline, drawn to the intersection of designed motion and world-building. Currently building out a personal world project, The Varokarn.
